Laboratory Services

Donation Testing Section

a) TTI Testing (Microbiology) Laboratory
Performs testing of Donor blood specimens for TTI markers of HIV, HBV, HCV and Syphilis infections.
b) Donor Grouping Laboratory
Performs testing of Donor blood specimens for ABO and Rh D grouping

Compatibility Testing Section (Blood Transfusion)

This section performs Pre Transfusion Testing on patient samples sent from various hospitals to ensure the compatibility of blood products issued for patients.

Hours of Operation

24 hours of the day including weekdays, weekends and public holidays

Histocompatibility Laboratory

Provides testing for Class I and Class II HLA by serologic techniques for purposes such as bone marrow and solid organ transplant donor /patient testing, disease association studies and paternity evaluation. Tests include HLA Class I & II typing, antibody screening/identification and cross matching.

Hours of Operation

Routine working hours - 8.00am – 4.00pm on week days
8.00am – 12.00pm on Saturdays

Reference Laboratory

Red Cell Immunohaematology (RCI) Reference Laboratory

Performs solving of Blood Group Serology problems referred by various hospital Blood Banks such as Blood Grouping anomalies, Compatibility testing problems. Also carries out special RCI tests like Red Cell Phenotyping, Antibody identification and titration, testing for Cold agglutinins etc.

Hours of Operation

Working hours - 8.00am -8.00pm on 365 days
